Covered Employees
Oregon state government does not have one central point that handles employment verifications. Each agency is responsible for its own employees.
The employee’s paystub is not an indicator of an employee’s agency, all State of Oregon employees' paystubs, will have the Department of Administrative Services, regardless of the agency they work for.
