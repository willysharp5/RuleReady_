The Worker Adjustment and Retraining Notification (WARN) Act is federal legislation that offers protection to workers, their families and communities by requiring employers to provide notice 60 days in advance of a covered-business closing and covered-business mass layoff. Enforcement of the WARN Act falls under U.S. Department of Labor jurisdiction. [Access the fact sheet on the WARN Act.](https://www.dli.pa.gov/Individuals/Workforce-Development/warn/Pages/requirements.aspx)
