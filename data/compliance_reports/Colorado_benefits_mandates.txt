In Colorado, nearly 900,000 workers do not have access to a retirement savings plan at work. This means many Coloradans are at risk of reaching retirement without the savings they need. The Colorado SecureSavings Program is a state-run retirement savings program that bridges this gap. It makes it easy for businesses to support their employees' financial future and gives every worker a chance to build a more secure future.
