# District of Columbia Break Laws
What is the break law in District of Columbia?

Covered Employers
In the District of Columbia, no state law requires employers to provide meal or rest breaks. However, employers can offer such breaks at their discretion, and adhere to the federal Fair Labor Standards Act (FLSA).
