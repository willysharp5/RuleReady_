Covered Employees
Employees in Maryland must be paid at least once every two weeks or twice in a month. However, Executive, Professional and Administrative employees may be paid less frequently (see the [discussion on Executive, Professional and Administrative employees,](https://dllr.state.md.us/labor/wagepay/wpsalaried.shtml)).
