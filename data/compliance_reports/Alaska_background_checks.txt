![New Icon](https://health.alaska.gov/dhcs/PublishingImages/new-icon.png)**Effective 11/01/2023**, the Department of Health and the Department of Family and Community Services are implementing new background check variance applications. Applicants seeking a variance for an employer/facility under the oversight of the Department of Health need to complete the Department of Health Background Check Variance Application. Applicants seeking a variance for foster care, adoption, or guardianship subsides, under the oversight of the Office of Children’s Services, need to complete the Department of Family and Community Services Background Check Variance Application. Copies of these applications can be found under the “ [All Users](https://health.alaska.gov/dhcs/Pages/cl/bgcheck/default.aspx#allusers)” section of this page.
