Coverage Election
1. If you do not have any employees who are eligible for the NC 401(k) Plan, beginning January 1, you will have the option to provide access to the NC 401(k) Plan to all of your employees. For example, if you are a local government that does not participate in LGERS or otherwise offer ae a defined benefit pension, you can elect to participate in the NC 401(k) Plan, which will allow all of your employees to save in the plan.
