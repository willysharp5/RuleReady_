# Nevada Pay Period and Frequency Laws

Covered Employers
Yes, there are laws in effect in Nevada that dictate pay period and frequency regulations. Most apply only to private employers.
## How Often Do Employers Need to Pay Employees in Nevada?
Under [NV Stat. 608.060](https://www.workforcehub.com/hr-laws-and-regulations/nevada/nevada-pay-period-and-frequency-laws/NV%20Statute%20608.060), private employers are required to pay all compensation and wages to employees semi-monthly. The law states that employers may pay employees more frequently than semi-monthly, but not less often.
