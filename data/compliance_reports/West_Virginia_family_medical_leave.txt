West Virginia Paid Family / Medical Leave (PFML)

Overview
As of January 3, 2025, West Virginia does not have a state-mandated Paid Family Leave (PFL) program for private-sector employees. Employees in West Virginia are covered under the federal Family and Medical Leave Act (FMLA), which provides unpaid, job-protected leave for qualifying family and medical reasons. Additionally, West Virginia has the Parental Leave Act (PLA) that offers unpaid leave for certain public employees.
