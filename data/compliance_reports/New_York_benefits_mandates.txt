Covered Employers
The New York State Secure Choice Savings Program (New York Secure Choice) is New York’s retirement savings program for private-sector employees who do not have access to a retirement plan at work. This new program provides an easy way for employees working in New York State to save for the future and is simple for employers to facilitate. Through automatic enrollment and payroll deduction, savers can contribute to Roth Individual Retirement Accounts (IRAs).
