California Minimum Wage

Overview
Starting April 1, 2024, all “fast food restaurant employees” who are covered by the new law must be paid at least $20.00 per hour.

Covered Employers
The minimum wage in California, effective January 1, 2025, is $16.50/hour for all employers. Fast Food Restaurant employers, effective April 1, 2024, and Healthcare Facility employers, effective October 16, 2024 (see below), have a higher minimum wage.
Fast food employers must post this Minimum Wage Order [Supplement for Fast Food Restaurant Employees](https://www.dir.ca.gov/iwc/MW-2024-FF-SUPPLEMENT.pdf).
