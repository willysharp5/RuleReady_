What Should Employers Do?
- [Skilled and trained contractor workforce requirements](https://dli.mn.gov/business/employment-practices/skilled-and-trained-contractor-workforce-requirements)
State law requires employers to provide employees with restroom time and sufficient time to eat a meal. If the break is less than 20 minutes in duration, it must be counted as hours worked.
Time to use the nearest restroom must be provided within each four consecutive hours of work. Meal time must be provided to employees who work eight or more consecutive hours. See Minnesota Statutes [177.253](https://www.revisor.mn.gov/statutes/?id=177.253) and [177.254](https://www.revisor.mn.gov/statutes/?id=177.254), and Minnesota Rules [5200.0120](https://www.revisor.mn.gov/rules/?id=5200.0120) for more information.
