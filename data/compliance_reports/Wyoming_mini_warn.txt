Wyoming Mini-WARN & Separation Notices

Overview
Employers with at least 100 employees are required to provide written notice 60 days before a mass layoff or closure. Wyoming does not have any additional requirements beyond what the U.S. Department of Labor (DOL) requires for a WARN notification to be issued.

Covered Employers
### Business Expansion/Relocation
The Wyoming Department of Workforce Services and its partners understand the issues associated with expanding and relocating a business. As such, we and our partners can assist your business with labor market information, recruitment, screening, training and a host of other human resource services. All at no cost!
