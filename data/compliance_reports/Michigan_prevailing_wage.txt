## State Project Contractor Requirements
Contractors and sub-contractors that employ construction mechanics must register annually with the Wage and Hour Division before bidding on or working on any State Project.

Covered Employees
_Construction mechanics_ include skilled or unskilled mechanics, laborers, workers, helpers, assistants, or apprentices performing physical construction work on state-funded projects.
Does not include: executive, administrative, professional, office, or custodial employees.
