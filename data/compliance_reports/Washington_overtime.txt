### Overtime rules for Washington workers
The Washington State Department of Labor & Industries in 2020 has updated its rules regarding the minimum salary threshold and job duties tests for salaried exempt employees defined as executive, administrative and professional as well as computer professionals. Changes were also made in the rules for exempt employees defined as outside salespeople.
