Special Requirements
For public background check purposes, please complete the [ALEA Application to Review Alabama Criminal History Record Information (CHRI).](https://www.alea.gov/sites/default/files/inline-files/SBI-ApplicationReviewALCHRI%20NEW_16.pdf) The applicant will have to be fingerprinted, provide a copy of the current photo ID, the AL CHRI application, and a payment of $25.00 in the form of a money order or Cashier’s check (each additional copy is $5.00 extra) in person at 301 S. Ripley Street, Montgomery, AL 36104 (Adams Ave side) or by mail at PO Box 1511, Montgomery, AL 36102-1511.  Please contact 334.676.7897 with any additional questions.
