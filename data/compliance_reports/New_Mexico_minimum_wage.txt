New Mexico Minimum Wage

Overview
New Mexico Department of Workforce Solutions’ (NMDWS) advises employers and workers that New Mexico’s minimum wage is increasing, effective Sunday, January 1st, 2023. This increase is in accordance with an amendment to the New Mexico Minimum Wage Act signed into law by Governor Lujan Grisham on April 1, 2019.
Employers are required to post the New Mexico Minimum Wage Act Summary Poster in a place where all workers can easily see it.
