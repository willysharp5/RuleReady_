Covered Employees
Paid Family Medical Leave (PFML) is a new benefit that provides eligible state employees with paid leave for certain qualifying FMLA events. Employees may qualify for up to forty hours per week for twelve weeks per year. PFML is intended to provide employees with peace of mind and financial security during life’s difficult moments.
