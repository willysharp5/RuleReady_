Virginia Pregnancy / Disability / ADA Accommodations

Overview
Pregnancy Workplace Accommodation Procedures The University of Virginia (“UVA”) is committed to providing accommodations, upon request, to ensure access to employment opportunities, benefits, programs, and services to qualified Employees who are pregnant or have pregnancy-related medical conditions arising out of pregnancy, childbirth, or related conditions, including lactation. These procedures apply to Academic Division and UVA Medical Center Employees. Applicants for UVA or UVA Health System employment should contact the UVA Office for Equal Opportunity and Civil Rights for additional information. NOTE: If an Employee has a pregnancy-related medical condition that qualifies as a disability, the Employee should consult UVA’s Procedures for Employees with Disabilities to Request Workplace Accommodations.
